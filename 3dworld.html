Deadpool Animation - Final Video

Animation Pipeline Breakdown

1. Concept & Storyboarding

Imagined the entire story and created rough storyboard panels to plan scenes, camera angles, and timing before animating.

2. 2D Design & Backgrounds

Hand-drew backgrounds and 2D character elements using Clip Studio Paint and Photoshop to create a consistent visual style blending with 3D models.

3. 3D Modeling

Modeled Deadpool and X-Men characters in ZBrush and Blender for detailed, animatable 3D assets.

4. Texturing

Painted realistic textures using Adobe Substance Painter and Photoshop to add detail and color to 3D models.

5. Rigging

Rigged characters in Blender and Mixamo to prepare for animation by creating bone structures and control rigs.

6. Animation

Animated characters in Blender, including hand-drawn dance scenes, bringing the story and action to life.

7. VFX & SFX

Created explosions, blood, and muzzle flashes in After Effects and Houdini to enhance the visual impact of action scenes.

8. Compositing & Editing

Combined all elements with color grading and sound syncing in After Effects and DaVinci Resolve for the polished final look.

9. Final Render & Export

Exported the completed animation in high quality, ready to showcase in portfolio and submissions.

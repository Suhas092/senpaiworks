About SenpaiWorks

Hello! I'm Suhas, the creator behind SenpaiWorks. I’m a B.Tech graduate in Electronics and Communication Engineering from PES University.

My journey into the world of animation and illustration began as a self-taught artist with a deep passion for storytelling and visual expression.

It all started when I brought my favorite characters to life through my own interpretations. Inspired by anime and the dynamic worlds of 2D/3D animation, I started creating art that resonated with fans.

What started as a hobby evolved into a platform for sharing creations and connecting with fellow art lovers. SenpaiWorks is a celebration of imagination, creativity, and storytelling.

Thanks for being part of this journey — let's keep creating magic together!
